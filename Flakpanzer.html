They were only produced in 1941 and used from 1941 - 1943, and only 24 were ever made. The Waffenamt (German Army Weapons Agency) decided to combine light anti-aircraft guns with the chassis of tanks. When they were in action they were folded down and used as something to stand on. They were armed with a Flak 38 AAG, and the crew were armed with Karabiner 98 k's. The battalion that the Flakpanzers were in was wiped out in the Battle of Stalingrad.
