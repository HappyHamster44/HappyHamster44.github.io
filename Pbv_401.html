Pbv 401

The Pbv 401 tank is a Swedish armored vehicle that was developed in the 1950s. It was designed to transport infantry troops across the battlefield while providing them with protection from small arms fire and other threats.
The Pbv 401 is a tracked vehicle, with a crew of two and a capacity for up to ten infantry soldiers. It is armed with a 20mm automatic cannon and a 7.62mm machine gun, providing both anti-armor and anti-personnel capabilities.
In addition to its role as a transport vehicle, the Pbv 401 can also be used for reconnaissance and fire support. Its armor and mobility make it well-suited for a variety of combat scenarios, and it has been used by the Swedish military in a number of conflicts over the years.
Overall, the Pbv 401 tank is a versatile and reliable armored vehicle that has served the Swedish military well for many decades.
