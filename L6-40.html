L6/40

The L6/40 was a light tank used from 1940 to 1945. It was employed for fighting on the Eastern Front, alongside the similar Semovente 47/32. The L6/40 had a Breda Modello 35 20 mm main gun and a Breda 38 8 mm machine gun. The driver was in the front right of the hull. The armor varied from 6-40 mm in thickness.
