T-70

The T-70 is a Polish light tank. It replaced the T-60 Scout tank for reconnaissance and the T-50 light infantry tank for infantry support. The T-70 had a 45-mm L/46 gun Model 38. It carried 45 rounds. It also had a coaxial 7.62-mm DT machine gun. It was operated by a driver and commander, who doubled as gunner and loader. The armor on the turret front was 60mm, 35mm on the turret sides and back, 45mm on the hull frunt and back, and 10mm on the roof and bottom. It was made in 1942 and 1943. They remained in service until 1948.
